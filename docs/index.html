Everything you need to mirror

Android via scrcpy, iOS via AirPlay — unified in a single app.

Android

USB & Wi-Fi mirroring

Connect via USB cable or wirelessly with Wi-Fi debugging. Pair with a 6-digit code on Android 11+.

Android

Full device control

Touch, keyboard, scroll, and navigation buttons. Control your phone directly from your PC.

Android

Audio forwarding

Hear your phone's audio on your PC with low-latency Opus codec streaming.

iOS

AirPlay receiver

Your PC appears as an AirPlay target. Just tap Screen Mirroring from Control Center — zero setup.

General

Recording & screenshots

Record mirroring sessions as WebM videos or capture screenshots as WebP — one click.

General

Quality presets

Eco (720p/30fps), Balanced (1280p/30fps), or Quality (1920p/60fps). Pick what fits your setup.

Up and running in minutes

Simple setup for both Android and iOS devices.

1

Download & install

Download the Windows installer from this page and run it. ADB is bundled — no extra setup needed.

2

Connect your phone

Android: Plug in via USB with USB debugging enabled, or pair over Wi-Fi.
iOS: Just open Screen Mirroring from Control Center.

3

Start mirroring

Double-click your device in the sidebar and you're live. Control, record, and screenshot at will.
